Vanilla (Banira?) is a clever and quiet catgirl who loves Chocola. She is Chocola twin sister and the youngest among the Minaduki catgirls. Vanilla is prone to be misunderstood because she rarely expresses her emotions. In fact, she is quite smart and often makes some remarkable insights. Her sharp wit and I will-do-things-my-way attitude make her Chocola complete opposite. However, she remains both kind-hearted and, well, catlike.
